About Me

Hello, my name is Monika Natius.
Welcome to my Portfolio! I have long been a creative soul. Honestly, it is what sustains me. I try and find beauty in all things and wish to make life more special in the ways that I can. I often find it is in the small details and personal touches - that have the most meaning. I have always been a hard worker and have never compromised originality and quality by taking the easy road. I believe in the value of craftsmanship and the importance of creating things that are made to last. I graduated with a Fine Arts degree in design and illustration. After many years working as Design Director for global brand leader Omnicom and cosmetics giant Revlon, I had taken a hiatis to care of my three children. I have three beautiful girls, including a set of twins. It was during these formative years, as I continued freelancing and curating creative events and parties for my family, that I created my own small business. Employing my own talents in marketing, designing, and determining the needs of my clients, proving my leadership and capability in the work force.

My designs are meant to be thoughtful reminders of events, celebrations and offer smiles in our all too often hectic world. A moment to reflect and cherish the sweet moments in life. It is the Danish concept of “Hygge”; which stresses the value of comfort, coziness and an overall sense of well being that inspires me. I am a determined, educated and passionate individual who believes in the power of creativity to bring joy and meaning to our lives. Driven towards the goal of creating beautiful and functional designs, I have a passion for bringing ideas to life through my work. I have a keen eye for detail and a strong understanding of design principles, which allows me to create visually appealing and effective designs. I am also a skilled photo editor, with experience in retouching, color correction, and image manipulation. My versatility allows me to be competent in various work environments, and I am always eager to take on new challenges and learn new skills.

If I sound like someone you would like to work with, please reach out!
